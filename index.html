Tech From The North

The Northern Tech Group is a technology conglomerate in the northern part of Nigeria, based in Kano, it is among the few technology conglomerate in northern Nigeria. The establishment of Northern Tech group was initated by a goal to create and improve technology companies specifically in the northern part of Nigeria, core business is to build technology startups and to provide cheaper internet access that will allow greater access to services that rely on internet.

Founders Mustapha Sulieman, Muhammad Abdullahi, Abdulrahaman Iliyasu, Abdulrahim Iliyasu and Hashim Aliyu.
